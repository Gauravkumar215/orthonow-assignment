No. Our surgeons try conservative care first — physiotherapy, posture correction and medication. Surgery is only ever recommended when it's genuinely the right call, and it's always your decision.

Yes. Most of our Bengaluru clinics offer evening slots, and we'll match you with one closest to your office so you don't lose a work day.
